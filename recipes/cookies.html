Chewy Cookies

Almond Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies (Classic Chewy):

These cookies are the epitome of keto cookie perfection – soft, chewy, and packed with rich chocolate chips. The secret ingredient? A combination of almond flour and coconut flour that mimics the texture of regular wheat flour without the carbs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup Swerve brown sugar sweetener
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together almond flour, coconut flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and sweetener until light and fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving about 2 inches of space between each cookie.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown around the edges and slightly soft in the center.
  7. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
